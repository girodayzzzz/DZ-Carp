Evening Carp Fishing at Soboško Jezero

10 March 2026 • Soboško jezero – Fishing Family Murska Sobota

Arrival at the Lake

After a busy day I decided to spend the evening fishing at Soboško jezero near Murska Sobota. The weather was calm and perfect for a relaxing carp fishing session. As I arrived at the lake the atmosphere was peaceful, with only a few anglers fishing along the banks.

The lake looked beautiful in the evening light and the water was almost completely calm. It was one of those sessions where simply being by the water already makes the trip worthwhile.

Choosing the Fishing Spot

Before setting up the rods I walked along the bank to find a good fishing position. Eventually I chose a spot with a clear view across the lake and enough open space to place the rods comfortably.

The water looked deeper further out and there were occasional movements on the surface that suggested fish activity somewhere in the area.

Fishing Setup

  • Rod: 12ft Carp Rod
  • Reel: Big pit reel
  • Rig: Simple hair rig
  • Bait: Sweet boilies

For this session I kept the setup simple. The rods were placed a few meters apart to cover more water and allow the bait to sit naturally on the lake bottom.

The Evening Session

As the evening progressed the lake became even quieter. The sun slowly began to set behind the trees and the light on the water changed beautifully.

Occasionally fish movement could be seen in the distance. Even though the rods stayed quiet for most of the evening, the peaceful surroundings made the whole session very enjoyable.

Fishing trips like this are not always about catching the biggest carp but about enjoying the nature, observing the water and learning more about the lake.

Lessons from the Session

This trip was a good opportunity to explore Soboško jezero and understand the lake a little better. Observing fish movement and water structure helps a lot when planning future fishing sessions.

Next time I visit the lake I will try slightly different spots and experiment with different baiting strategies.

Conclusion

Even though this was a short evening session, it was a great way to spend time outdoors and enjoy the calm environment around Soboško jezero.

I will definitely return to this lake again for another carp fishing trip and hopefully share more catches and adventures here on DZ Carp.
